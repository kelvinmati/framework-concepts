Ed-tech

EdTech, short for education technology, encompasses the use of technology to enhance learning experiences. From e-books and digital materials to interactive whiteboards and virtual classrooms, EdTech aims to transform traditional teaching methods, making education more accessible and tailored to individual needs.

By accommodating various learning styles and speeds, EdTech opens new doors for personalised learning, not only in Africa but globally.
