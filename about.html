Meet The Founder.

Taakila Afrika is a Kenyan entrepreneur and innovator. He is the founder of Framework Concepts, a digital solutions company that provides services to businesses and organizations across Africa. Taakila is passionate about using technology to solve real-world problems and to create opportunities for Africans.Taakila is a graduate of Moi University, where he studied Finanace and Banking. After graduating, he went to E-mobilis school of technology before starting his own business in 2022

Framework Concepts has quickly become one of the leading digital solutions companies in Africa. The company provides a wide range of services, including web development,mobile app development, software development. Framework Concepts has worked with a variety of clients, including government agencies, businesses, and non-profit organizations.

Taakila is a frequent speaker at tech events and conferences. He is also a mentor to young entrepreneurs and innovators. He is passionate about helping others to succeed and to use technology to make a positive impact on the world.

Taakila is also a passionate advocate for using technology to solve real-world problems and to create opportunities for Africans.
